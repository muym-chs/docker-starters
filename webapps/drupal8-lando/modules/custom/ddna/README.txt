Drupal DNA (DDNA)

INTRODUCTION
============
Module can help chase your business grow fast as possible using online tools.
Inside your Drupal DNA build automatic documentation to reduce cost and see what is inside, specification to your project will be updated with the clock configured with CRON scheduler.
Connect DNA module to your Drupal instance and share with your colleagues to save time and bring more projects to the life.

One click to connect with spreadsheet, just add token and ready to go.
Behat testing included.

REQUIREMENTS
============
Requires Drupal core config module.

INSTALLATION
============
Installs as usually Drupal module installation via Administration
or through `composer require drupal/ddna`.
